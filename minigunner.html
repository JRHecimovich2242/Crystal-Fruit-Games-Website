MINIGUNNER

Do you have what it takes?

Stranded on a frigid mining outpost, you must protect the reactor that powers your distress beacon from hordes of gloops- squishy slimes that attack with plasma projectiles! Collect items, set up sentries, defeat bosses, and fight to survive in this twin-stick wave-defense roguelike.

Play Now!

FIGHT

Defend the outpost from relentless swarms of enemies. Play from a cast of grizzled mercenaries, each with unique weapons, items, and abilities! Dodge attacks by combat rolling and make every bullet count!

BUY

Spend scrap at shops to buy curious items and increase your fighting power. Pick up items that synergize well with each other to create a limitless number of unique builds. Take your chances with the store's random option to get legendary items for cheap. Complete challenging achievements to unlock new items, playable characters, and more!

PREPARE

Adapt to survive as enemies break through barricades and find new paths to attack from. Utilize teleporters, vents, doors, and a drawbridge to control the flow of combat and give yourself an edge in battle. You can also spend scrap on sentry turrets and automated drones to bolster your defenses!

SURVIVE

Shoot, dodge, and blast your way to victory as you defend the outpost's reactor until reinforcements arrive.
